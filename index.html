Our Story

The Indonesian Catholic Community (ICC) at Austin was founded in September 15, 2002. Members of ICC Austin consist of students, professionals and families with Indonesian background or interest who lives in Austin, Texas Our community has grown over the years. With the grace of God and supportive members, ICC Austin has been able to host spiritual, volunteer as well as fun activities such as: monthly rosary, Indonesian mass, annual Easter celebration, Mobile Loves and Fishes (MLF) and more.
